struggle

The term 'struggle' has layers of meaning,
not only encompassing the act of struggling
but also the broader concepts of conflict
and hardship. This becomes particularly
poignant when comparing the struggles
faced by the rich and the poor.
For the wealthy, their struggles are likened
to being in a misty cloud; they have the luxury
to choose whether to soar high or to rest leisurely.
Even in their hardest efforts, they remain cushioned
in their cloud-like environment. This metaphor
contrasts sharply with the more stark and often
harsh realities faced by the poor in their struggles.
It underscores that although the word 'struggle'
is used for both, the experiences are vastly different.
